Common Questions

What counts as a "client"?
Each business entity you manage is one client. Smith Plumbing Pty Ltd = 1 client. If the same director has 3 businesses, that's 3 clients. You pay per entity, not per person.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The 14-day free trial is completely free with no credit card required. If you don't upgrade, your account expires automatically — no surprise charges, no action needed from you.
Can I add or remove clients mid-month?
Yes. Add clients any time and you're charged pro-rata for remaining days. Remove a client and billing stops at the next cycle. You always pay for only what you use.
Where is my data stored?
100% in Australia on AWS Sydney (ap-southeast-2). We're Privacy Act 1988 compliant. Your client data never leaves Australia and we'll never sell or share it with third parties.
Does the Xero integration cost extra?
No. Xero integration is included for all clients. MYOB integration is coming in a future update — also included at no extra cost.
How do I cancel?
Cancel from Settings → Billing in under 30 seconds. No lock-in, no penalties, no questions asked. Your data is available to export for 30 days after cancellation.
